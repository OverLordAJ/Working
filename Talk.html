We Need to Talk

Zorblax: We need to talk.

Klyptra: Ugh, what now? Did I leave slime on the console again?

Zorblax: No, it's about the Earth probe data. You've been watching those human "reality TV" signals for three cycles straight.

Klyptra: And? They're fascinating. The one with the singing competition? Pure chaos energy.

Zorblax: You're supposed to be analyzing atmospheric composition, not learning human dance moves. I saw you trying the floss yesterday.

Klyptra: Okay fine, guilty. But in my defense, tentacles are surprisingly good at it.

Zorblax: It's distracting the whole crew. Half the ship is doing the floss now. Productivity is down 47%.

Klyptra: Look, maybe humans are onto something. Maybe we need more… vibes.

Zorblax: Vibes won't refuel the warp core.

Klyptra: True. But vibes make long voyages less boring. Admit it—you kinda liked the cat videos I showed you.

Zorblax: …The small furry ones are acceptable. Fine. You can keep watching. But limit it to one cycle per shift.

Klyptra: Deal. And I'll share the best ones. We can have a viewing party.

Zorblax: Only if there's no more dancing in the engine room.

Klyptra: No promises. But I'll try. You're the best co-pilot, you know that?

Zorblax: Yeah yeah. Now get back to actual work… after one more cat video.
